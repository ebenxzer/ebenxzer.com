Goodfoot

I worked as a freelancer at Goodfoot, a development agency based in NYC where I was responsible for all things UX, and UI related. My client, Doc-Mate, was keen on disrupting the trucking industry and make freight shipment easier for truck drivers, site owners, and other parties involved to communicate with each other, keep track of shipment in real-time, and access delivery sites independently through a mobile app. For 3 months, I periodically met with the project manager and the client, worked on the UI, and prototyped the designs using InVision.
