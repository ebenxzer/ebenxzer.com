Redesigning My Portfolio

March 24, 2020Updated April 1, 2020

With the "Rona" running rampant across the globe, I'm using my time indoors to create this blog as a means to practice web development and to document the progress of transforming this site into a portfolio of which I have full control over.

Why are you doing this?

For years, I've been struggling with the crippling need to be perfect paired with procrastination. I want to show my progress and more than anything, build for fun and keep myself accountable by creating in the "public". My primary goal is to showcase my technical chops to go with my design skills.
